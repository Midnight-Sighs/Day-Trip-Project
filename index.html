Travel Fantasy

Here at Travel Fantasy, we take care of all your travel needs! We book the location, we book your fun adventures, and even give you a reservation at an eatery of our choice! Don't worry, we know all the best places to try!

View your options below, but remember, WE know what's best. So we'll do all the work of planning your itinerary for you! We hope you've traveled by interdimensional wormholes before...because if not....you're in for a doozy!

Sword Coast

Of all our travel options, going to the Sword Coast gives you the best chance to meet a variety of people! Here you can get back to basics, and you may even go home with the gift - an authentic case of polio or smallpox! Take home lasting memories of your grand vacation! We offer a variety of ways to get around, reccomend places to eat, and there are a ton of things to do for fun!

The Normandy

The Normandy is a great vacation location if you really want to get "out of this world". The Normandy is fun and free and the odds of the ship going into combat while you're aboard are really low (so we hear) so you can be sure you'll be safe. The ship is fully stocked with food and the crew is all aboard and available for your every need at any time! They're there for YOU!

P.S. Don't worry about the flashing red lights!

Gold Saucer

The Gold Saucer is full of sights, sounds, and smells! We hear the smell of Chocobo isn't the greatest, but the rest of the Gold Saucer is certainly worth the trip! Make sure you bring PLENTY of extra pocket change (after you're settled with us, of course) to place bets and play games while you're there! The Gold Saucer isn't cheap but it is an experience you'll never forget!!
